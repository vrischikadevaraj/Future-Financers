Resources


Tools

Calculators

Interactive calculator tools can help students in calculating budgets, savings, compound interest growth, and investment returns.

Investing Simulations

Beginner friendly investing platforms that simulate real stocks and portfolio building, without risking money.

Credit & Loans

Educational resources that explain credit, loans and interest rates.
Practice building credit score and repaying loans from an early age to build good money habits.



Khan Academy

A free educational platform that offers a wide range of interactive lessons on personal finance, with varying knowledge levels and practice questions.

Next Gen Personal Finance (NGPF)

A nonprofit organization that strives to improve financial literacy for middle and high schoolers through interactive lessons and an educator-approved curriculum.

OpenStax

A nonprofit educational initiative by Rice University that provides free, peer-reviewed textbooks and learning materials on economics and personal finance.

Investopedia

An online finance resource that holds definitions, explanations and real-world examples of financial ideas.

All of these resources are freely available to all students and can provide a wealth of financial knowledge. Together, they can support students' financial literacy education and strengthen their confidence and ability in making informed financial decisions now and in the future.
