Risk vs Reward

With every risk there are varying rewards that coudl be gained. Risk is the chance that you could lose money and reward is what you might gain in return. Generally higher potential reward comes with higher risk

Investment Types:

Stocks

Stocks mean that you can buy a small piece (a "share") of a company. The more the company grows the share can increase in value. Certain companies can be volatile and the stock price can go up and down.

Real Estate

Real estate is investing into property. It is possible to earn money through rent, and the land property value that can change over time. Real estate can be stable and reliable than investments, but it sometimes requires a greater amount of money to invest in.

Crypto

Cryptocurrency is a digital asset that is traded online. It can have high reward potential but is often very volatile. It is often recommended that people should only invest money that they can afford to lose.
